  1. Undamaged or damaged structural/non-structural BBC
  2. Close-ups of damaged areas on such a structure
  3. Complete side or front views of the building

Note that you need to answer this question in each image within 2~3 seconds. Please make decision based on a major visual evidence in the images and do not focus on tiny detail (e.g. small building on the background). Here, "No" images include corrupted images such as blurred or strange angles regardless of relevant visual contents.
